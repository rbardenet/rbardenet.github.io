A determinantal point process is a distribution over clouds of points, where the points repell each other. Realizations typically look like well-spread point patterns. DPPs have strangely appeared in various domains, such as quantum physics, random matrix theory, or number theory. Recently, they have appeared in stats, and I am investigating their use.
