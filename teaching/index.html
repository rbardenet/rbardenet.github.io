Teaching

I am passionate about teaching. In 2016-2017, I am teaching two master-level courses:

  • Bayesian nonparametrics 101 at ENSAE Paris. The objective is that students can read and understand recent advances in BNP research.
  • Machine learning applied to bankruptcy prediction at Ecole Centrale de Lille. This course is built around an ML competition on bankruptcy prediction, and aims at preparing future data scientists to face the wild data world out there.
